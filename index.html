Welcome to my website! I am a fifth-year PhD student at UCLA Anderson School of Management. My advisor is Professor Velibor Mišić. My research interests lie at the intersection of consumer choice modeling, behavioral decision making, revenue mananagement, and machine learning. A list of my publications can be found below.

I will be on the 2021-22 academic job market.
