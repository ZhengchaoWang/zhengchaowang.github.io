I am a PhD candidate at Imperial College Business School, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Wolfram Wiesemann and Professor Heikki Peura.

My research lies at the intersection of analytics for social goods, revenue management, optimization, and statistical learning. It aims to make intelligent decision in face of uncertainty and to gain information efficiently.
