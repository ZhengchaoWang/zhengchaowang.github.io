About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a fourth-year PhD student of Decisions, Operations, and Technology Management at UCLA Anderson School of Management. My advisor is Professor Velibor Mišić.

My research interests lie at the intersection of consumer choice models (including irrational choice), assortment optimization, data-driven analytics, and machine learning. A list of publications can be found below or on my google scholar.
