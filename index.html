Welcome to my website! My name is Yi-Chun Chen and I am a third-year PhD student at UCLA Anderson School of Management. My advisor is Professor Velibor Mišić. I joined UCLA in September 2017. Before that, I was a graduate student in ICME at Stanford University from 2014 to 2017 (advised by Professor Mykel Kochenderfer), and an undergraduate student in physics at National Taiwan University from 2009 to 2013.

My research interests lie at the intersection of operations research, machine learning, and management. A list of publications can be found below or on my google scholar.
