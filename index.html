About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a fifth-year PhD student at UCLA Anderson School of Management, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Velibor Mišić. My research interests include consumer choice modeling, behavioral decision making, revenue management, data-driven analytics, and optimization. Before joining UCLA, I received a MS in Computational and Mathematical Engineering from Stanford University, and a BS in Physics from National Taiwan University.
