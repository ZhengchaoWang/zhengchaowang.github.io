Welcome to my website! I am a fifth-year PhD student at UCLA Anderson School of Management, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Velibor Mišić. My research interests include consumer choice modeling, behavioral decision making, revenue management, data-driven analytics, and optimization. A list of my publications can be found below.
